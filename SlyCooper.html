Sly 2 Band of Thieves

Top Left
  • Release date: September 14, 2004
  • ESRB rating: E
  • Tags: 3d platformer, stealth, action
  • Studio: Sucker Punch

Sly 2 Band of Thieves is and 3d platformer that is considered on of the best stealth games of all time and one of the best Playstation 2 games of all time. After the success of the studio Sucker Punch's Sly Cooper Thievius Raccoonus they began working on the sequal to follow up the series. They made major improvements such as adding a health bar, better graphics, and overhaled the core game play.

The story is about a Raccoon named Sly and has to steal the body parts of Clockwerk and destroy them, but before his crew can steal the parts he is caught by the police. He then finds out another gang has stolen the parts and goes on a mission to steal them back.

The player jumps, climbs, and sneak their way through missions. There is the occational mini game ranging from shooting down drones to dancing in ball in a ballroom to distract a crowd. The characters have amazing personalities which makes interacting with them a joy. The sound track makes the enviornments more immersive. This is a great start to the massive library the Playstation 2 has.
