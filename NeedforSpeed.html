Need For Speed Hot Pursuit 2

Details

Release date:
September 30, 2002
ESRB rating:
Everyone (E)
Tags:
Driving, Racing, Action
Studio:
Electronic Arts (EA)
Number of players:
1-2 players

Electronic Arts is well known today for all of its sports games such as Madden, FIFA, and NBA. In the early 2000s EA worked on smaller titles to make ends meet. The Need for Speed series was one of these smaller titles.

Need for Speed Hot Pursuit 2 is a racing game focus on driving fancy cars while being chased by the police. The driving is fast paced and controls well with drifting controls. The sense of speed it immersive with the shaking screen when the cars are up to speed. There is a campaign to unlock new cars and become the fastest racer there ever was. The game gives the player the opportunity to be the cops and run other racers off the road. Also, this game has split screen so a player and their buddy can race together or work on opposites ends of the law. The game is very enjoyable to everyone and accessable to all skill levels.
