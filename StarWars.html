Star Wars Battlefront 2 (2005)

Top Left
  • Release date: October 28, 2005
  • ESRB rating: T
  • Tags: Action, First Person Shooter
  • Studio: Pandemic

In 2005 Lucasfilms were making the Star Wars prequels. With the final entry of the being released it was a perfect time to release a new video game. Pandemic released Star Wars Battlefront to amazing reviews they got the go ahead to make the next entry. This gave Pandemic the opportunity to add to the new entry.

The game is focused events from the movies and battles mentioned. There is a story mode that follows the 501st from the whole Star Wars series. This spans from battles on plants to battles in space between 2 ships in an effort for control. These battles feel massive in scale and you play as a regular soldier in these battles.

The gameplay consist of shooting enemies and taking control of command post. You have a variety of classes to choose from ranging from snipers, regular solders, to demolitionist that fire rocket launchers. A new feature is that players could take control of famous charaters from the Star Wars series. Play as Luke Skywaker and slice through Stormtroopers or play as Boba Fett and fly around using his jet pack. The game also give the option to make your own scenarios that you can play out. The game also supports split screen so you and a buddy can play together. With so much variety this is hours of fun.
