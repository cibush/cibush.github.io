Tom Clancy was an author that make some of the best selling books. Some of his work included "The Hunt for Red October", "Rainbow Six", and "Jack Reacher" series. He sold the video game rights to Ubisoft and made some of the best military shooters.

One of the first games was Splinter Cell. The game revolves around the player controling a spy named Sam Fisher as he is tasked with handleing threats to national security. As the story progresses the missions become more dangerous and the player has to over come these obstacls.

The player is given a pistol to shot enemies, night vision goggles to see in the dark, and dynamic movement for the player to master. A captivating story and great gameplay, this will be a hard game to put down once you start.
